Steps to successfully register your Agency -

Step 1:
Go to http://travmaks.in/accounts/seller/account/signup and fill up the form.
Make sure you have uploaded the currect ID proof, by clicking the preview link.
Read the Terms and Conditions, and click the checkbox to agree with.
Then submit by clicking the submit button.
Step 2:
You will get an email from us soon.
Open the email and click the confirm button to confirm your Account.
Then you will be redirected to the Log In page.
For any query and doubt, feel free to Contact Us.
Step 3:
Now fill the form with the right credencials.
Then Press Login button to Log In.
Step 4:
You will be redirected to the Agency Registration page. Fill the form with your agency details and click on submit button to Register your agency.
Welcome to the Travmaks family. It may take some time to verify your details. Kindly wait, we will contact to you as soon as possible.
